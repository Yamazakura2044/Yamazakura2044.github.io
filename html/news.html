Testimonials of Individuals that survived their first semester of college.
"There was a time in my life where I thought "Wow, maybe this really is the path for me." Day by day I think of regret whenever I speak those lines. I truly was not prepared for this, nothing in my years in highschool prepared me for what was to come. They were all insane in the head, thinking back now. All high and mightily talking about their degrees. But whatever. I've survived, that's what matters."- Anonymous "I remember fondly when we finished our finals, how the feeling amongst my classmates were not a real sense of relief that it's over, but the dread knowing that it was not. I sympathize with them really, most of their families worked hard to get them to where they are now. It's immense pressure trying to live up to something you know you're not. I should know, I do."- Anonymous "I've never been more stressed in my entire life. I definitely underestimated college to the highest degree. After all, I came here expecting to be all high and mighty like I always was back in highschool. But it was different this time. I was not able to handle it as much as I could. I found my limit, academically, and passionately. I've noticed people that could do what I can, but better, more accomplished and. I don't know, it broke me. I guess. After all, what am I if not the things that I frequently prided myself for. Hopefully by this break and by next sem, I'd figure myself out." - Anonymous
