Stella Navis and The Tesla Drive The Outerworld Colonies
The Spaceship of the Eighteenth Century. The Stella Navis was created by the
great inventor, Nikola Tesla to traverse the stars. It was powered primarily
by the Tesla Drive which cut the needed time of space travel by half.
It allowed us to get to Mars in less than a month, a speed unparalleled even
to the age of exploration's ships and travel. With the then recent advancements
made by Herr Tesla that sponsored the drive towards space colonization, the
great colonization of Mars started. Since then, numerous revisions has been
done towards the Stella Navis, to the point that a "Stella Navis" is just a classification.
The Tesla Drive remains as a constant. Even after Nikola Tesla's death.		 Shortly after the discovery of the Tesla Drive and
the usage of it on Stella Navis, colonies were made
all throughout the various planets and moons in our solar system.
